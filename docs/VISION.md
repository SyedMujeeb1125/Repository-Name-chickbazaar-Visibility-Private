# Product Vision

## Product

ChickBazaar is an autonomous B2B poultry supply-chain operating system operated by FruitGlobe International Private Limited. It is not an e-commerce marketplace and retailers do not browse farms.

## Mission

Make poultry procurement predictable, private, automated, and operationally reliable for retailers, farms, drivers, and ChickBazaar teams.

## Operating Model

1. Retailers submit demand to ChickBazaar.
2. Farms publish available live-bird inventory to ChickBazaar.
3. ChickBazaar locks demand, allocates supply, procures birds, plans logistics, and delivers to retailers.
4. ChickBazaar owns the commercial relationship and protects both sides from identity leakage.
5. Humans intervene primarily for exceptions and authorized overrides.

## Applications

### Retailer App

Registration, OTP login, ordering, recurring demand, future bookings, contracts, tracking, invoices, ledger, payments, notifications, and support.

### Farm App

Registration, inventory, allocation acceptance, pickup/loading confirmation, performance, and payment history.

### Driver App

Pickup and delivery schedules, navigation, actual weight, POD, collections, and completion.

### Admin ERP

Control Tower, sales, procurement, logistics, finance, operations, analytics, settings, audit, and exception management.

## Strategic Principles

- Automation by default; intervention by exception.
- Privacy between marketplace sides is a product invariant.
- Operational truth comes from state transitions and audit events, not editable status labels.
- Repeat orders and contracts create future demand before fulfillment dates.
- Financial records are immutable after posting and corrected through adjustments.
- Multi-city capability is designed into the data model from the beginning.

## Release Vision

- Version 1: complete transactional workflows for all four roles.
- Version 2: demand planning and operational Control Tower.
- Version 3: multi-city operations, GPS, and dynamic allocation.
- Version 4: national expansion, enterprise analytics, and partner networks.
