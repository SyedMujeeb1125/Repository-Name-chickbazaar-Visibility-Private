# 1. Executive Summary

ChickBazaar is a B2B poultry commerce platform that connects poultry farms with retail chicken shops through a digital ordering and fulfilment system.

Version 1 focuses exclusively on live broiler chicken procurement and delivery.

The platform enables retailers to place orders digitally, farms to fulfil them efficiently, drivers to deliver them, and administrators to manage the complete supply chain from one platform.

---

# 2. Vision

To become India's most trusted poultry commerce and supply chain platform.

---

# 3. Mission

Simplify poultry procurement through technology by providing transparent pricing, dependable fulfilment, and efficient operations.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Today, poultry retailers face several operational challenges:

• Calling multiple farms every morning.
• Uncertain pricing.
• No live inventory visibility.
• Manual order tracking.
• Credit management through paper records.
• No standardized procurement process.

ChickBazaar solves these challenges through a unified digital platform.

# 6. Product Scope (Version 1)

The first version includes:

• Retailer Mobile App
• Admin Portal
• Farm Portal
• Driver App
• Live Chicken Ordering
• Smart Allocation
• Payment Management
• Order Tracking
• Invoice Management
• Ledger Management

---

# 7. Out of Scope (Version 1)

The following features are intentionally excluded from Version 1:

• Eggs
• Processed Chicken
• Hotel Ordering
• Restaurant Ordering
• AI Forecasting
• Own Farms
• Processing Plants
• Multi-City Operations

These features remain part of the long-term roadmap.

---

# 8. Success Criteria

Version 1 will be considered successful when:

• 100 Retailers Registered
• 20 Farm Partners
• Daily Active Orders
• Reliable Daily Operations
• Positive Customer Feedback
• Stable Platform
